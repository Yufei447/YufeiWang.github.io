About Me

As a Chinese with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, I acquired fundamental computer knowledge such as operating systems, computer architecture, and data management during my college years. I began my programming journey in my freshman year and achieved an average academic score of 89.63%[transcript] during my college studies.

Being deeply obsessed with the digital age and its transformative impact on various industries through data utilization, I embarked on an internship at Zhilian Zhaopin, a leading online recruitment platform in China. There, I learned how to utilize huge amount of data to generate business insights.

I pursue farther education at University of Southern California, majoring applied data science. Willing to take challenges, I took many valuable courses such as optimization skills and data mining.

Field of Interests

Driven by my keen interest in the recommendation system and its profound influence on people's lives, I learned it by myself. I learned many classical methods, including collaborative filtering, matrix factorization, as well as models emphasizing feature interactions such as Poly2 and its modified versions like FM and FFM.

With the advent of the deep learning era, AutoRec and Deep Crossing focus on representing of user and item characteristics using neural network. And NeuralCF and PNN focus more on feature interactions to enhance data representation. Wide & Deep was introduced from a business perspective and I also learned its variations like Deep & Cross and Deep FM.

The introduction of attention mechanisms in recommendation systems, starting with AFM. And Alibaba built DIN and DIEN for their e-commerce platform.

I’m thrilled about this ever-evolving field and remain dedicated to learning more. Consequently, in the summer of 2023, I undertook an internship [hyperlink] with Ping’an Science & Technology Company to develop an AI-driven recommendation assistant for insurance agents based on Large Language Model.
